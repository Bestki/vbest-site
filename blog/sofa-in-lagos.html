Why choose a luxury sofa?

A quality sofa is an investment — it affects comfort, the look of your home, and long-term value. Luxury sofas typically have:

  • Sturdy hardwood frames and high-density foam
  • Durable upholstery (leather or performance fabrics)
  • Professional finishing and warranty options

Top sofa styles Lagos buyers love

  • Italian leather sofas: Elegant, easy-to-clean, and long-lasting.
  • Sectionals / L-shape sofas: Great for bigger living rooms and family spaces.
  • 3-seater & classic sofas: Timeless and space-efficient for most Lagos homes.
  • Recliners & modular sofas: Ideal for home cinemas and flexible layouts.

How to choose the right size

Measure your living area before you buy:

  1. Measure the wall or area where your sofa will sit.
  2. Allow at least 45–60cm of walking space in front of the sofa in busy rooms.
  3. Consider doorways/stairs — make sure the sofa can be carried into your home.

For a typical Lagos apartment: a comfortable 3-seater is usually 200–220cm wide; an L-shape sectional commonly spans 240–320cm depending on configuration.

Best materials for Lagos weather

Lagos humidity and daily life demand durable and easy-to-clean upholstery:

  • Top-grain leather: Durable, ages well, easy to wipe clean.
  • Performance fabrics: Polyester blends treated for stain resistance.
  • Quick-dry cushions: Useful if you live near the coast — choose foam with open-cell or fiber wrap.

Care tips — keep your sofa looking new

  • Vacuum fabric regularly and spot-clean spills immediately.
  • Apply leather conditioner once or twice a year for leather sofas.
  • Rotate cushions monthly to prevent uneven wear.
