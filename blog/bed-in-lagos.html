Types of Luxury Beds

Tufted Upholstered & Velvet Beds

Tufted beds are a signature luxury look—deep buttoning, plush upholstery, and a tall headboard. They work best in classic and contemporary rooms where comfort and visual impact are priorities.

Platform & Minimalist Beds

Platform beds offer a low-profile modern look; excellent for minimal interior design. Many platform models include integrated storage drawers or a slatted base for mattress breathability.

Storage / Ottoman Beds

These beds hide storage under the mattress: great for Lagos apartments where storage matters. Look for robust hydraulic lifts and reinforced frames.

Four-Poster & Canopy Beds

For a dramatic, hotel-style bedroom, four-poster and canopy beds create scale and presence—use sparingly in smaller rooms.

Choosing the Right Bed Size for Your Room

Measure your room before buying. Recommended clearances: at least 60–90cm (2–3ft) on the sides and foot of the bed for comfortable movement.

  • Single/Twin — 90cm x 190cm: for kids, small rooms.
  • Double/Full — 135cm x 190cm: compact couples or guest rooms.
  • Queen — 150cm x 200cm: popular choice for bedrooms.
  • King — 180cm x 200cm: luxurious, best for master bedrooms.

Also consider mattress thickness and bedframe footprint. Account for bedside tables and door swing.

How to Check Bed Frame & Mattress Quality

Frame & Joinery

Prefer solid hardwood or metal frames. Avoid thin particleboard or hollow sections. Inspect joints: dovetail, mortise-and-tenon or reinforced bolts are signs of quality.

Suspension & Slats

Check slat thickness and spacing. Wider, stronger slats increase mattress life. Some luxury beds use sprung slat systems for extra comfort.

Mattress: Foam vs Spring

High-density polyurethane foam (≥30 kg/m³) offers durability. Pocket-sprung mattresses provide targeted support and airflow. Hybrid mattresses combine both—often the best choice for luxury and support.

Upholstery & Finish

Inspect fabric grain, stitching and foam backing. Genuine leather or top-grade fabrics last longer and photograph better for resale value.

Styling Your Bedroom Around a Luxury Bed

Build a cohesive palette: choose two main colors and one accent. Use textured bedding, layered throws and cushions. Match bed height with bedside table scale; aim for balanced proportions.

Lighting

Install warm, dimmable bedside lighting and a central fixture that complements the bed’s style. Sconces free up bedside space and give a hotel-like touch.

Flooring & Rugs

A rug under the bed anchors the layout—choose offset placement so the rug extends beyond the bed on three sides.

Buying, Delivery & Warranty — Practical Tips

  • Buy from verified sellers: read reviews, ask for showroom visits, request real customer photos.
  • Delivery: confirm delivery cost, lead time, and assembly service. For Lagos, check urban access and apartment elevator fit.
  • Warranty: get written warranty for frame and mattress (1–5 years typical).
  • Returns & Trial: If mattress trials are offered, use them. Verify return policy for bedframes (custom pieces often final sale).

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I buy a bed online with confidence?

Yes — buy from reputable sellers, ask for detailed specs and customer images, and confirm return/trial policies before purchase.

How do I maintain a luxury bed?

Rotate the mattress per manufacturer guidelines, vacuum upholstered frames, avoid direct sunlight on fabrics, and tighten bolts yearly.

Do you offer custom sizes?

Many high-end suppliers offer made-to-measure beds—ask about lead time and sample fabrics before ordering.
